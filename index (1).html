  • Microwave

Things You Will Need

Ingredients

  • Ramen
  • Spice
  • Vegetable Packet

Ramen Recipe

Directions

  1. Open ramen and spice packet.
  2. Mix spices and vegetables into ramen.
  3. Add water.
  4. Put in microwave. Turn on for 3 minutes.
  5. Remove from microwave. Let cool for 1 minute.
  6. Eat.
